# Answer: Topple the Tower

## Primary Tower

**Class: Warrior**

**Path (left/right choices at each floor):**

| Floor | Choice | Encounter |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 2 | Left | Enchanted Shield (treasure) |
| 3 | Left | Campfire |
| 4 | Right | Jaw Worm (enemy) |
| 5 | Right | Campfire |
| 6 | Right | Campfire |
| 7 | Left | Campfire |
| 8 | Left | Campfire |
| 9 | — | The Collector (boss) |

**Estimated win probability: ~95%**

### Reasoning

The key insight is that items are class-specific. Enchanted Shield is a Warrior-class item that dramatically boosts Warrior survival. Warriors are immune to Jaw Worm, so the only real threat is the boss. With 1 own-class item and only 1 enemy fought, Warriors have a ~95% chance of defeating The Collector. The 4 campfires along the way provide additional safety margin.

## Bonus Tower (Ascension 20)

**Class: Warrior**

**Path (left/right choices at each floor):**

| Floor | Choice | Encounter |
|-------|--------|-----------|
| 2 | Right | Gremlin (enemy) |
| 3 | Right | Jaw Worm (enemy) |
| 4 | Right | Adamant Armor (treasure) |
| 5 | Left | Enchanted Shield (treasure) |
| 6 | Left | Sentries (enemy) |
| 7 | Left | Campfire |
| 8 | Left | Vanishing Powder (treasure) |
| 9 | — | The Champion (boss) |

**Estimated win probability: ~86%**

### Reasoning

The Champion is the hardest boss, but Warriors with 2 own-class items (Adamant Armor + Enchanted Shield) have only ~13% death rate against it. Gremlin and Jaw Worm are harmless to Warriors, and Sentries with 2 own-class items are nearly safe. The Vanishing Powder is a Rogue item (not helpful), but picking it up as a treasure is better than fighting an enemy in that slot.
